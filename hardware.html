Pikashow F8 Pro Flight Controller

A sleek, compact design with a matte black PCB. It has gold-plated solder pads for enhanced connectivity and durability. The controller includes multiple ports for various sensors and peripherals, including USB-C for easy firmware updates and configuration. The board is adorned with the Pikashow logo and model name in white, ensuring clear brand identification.

General Hardware Details

  • Processor: High-performance STM32F7 microcontroller
  • IMU: Dual IMU sensors (accelerometer and gyroscope)
  • Connectivity: USB-C, multiple UART ports, I2C, SPI
  • Power Management: 7V - 42V input range, integrated PDB, voltage and current sensing
  • Additional Features: Built-in OSD, Betaflight/Cleanflight support, LED port, barometer, SD card slot

Cube Orange+ Flight Controller

The Cube Orange+ Flight Controller is a cutting-edge flight control system designed for professional and industrial drone applications. Equipped with an STM32H7 microcontroller and triple redundant IMU sensors, it ensures exceptional processing power and reliability. With a wide range of connectivity options, including USB-C, CAN, and multiple UART ports, it offers versatility for various peripherals and sensors. The dual power inputs and integrated voltage and current sensing provide robust power management. Additional features like the built-in ADS-B receiver, high-speed SD card slot for data logging, and redundant barometers make the Cube Orange+ a comprehensive solution for complex flight missions, compatible with both ArduPilot and PX4 flight stacks. Its compact design and lightweight make it ideal for a wide range of UAV applications.

General Hardware Details

  • Processor: STM32H7 microcontroller
  • IMU: Triple redundant IMU sensors
  • Connectivity: USB-C, multiple UART ports, CAN, I2C, SPI
  • Power Management: Dual power inputs, wide voltage range, voltage and current sensing
  • Additional Features: Built-in ADS-B receiver, high-speed SD card slot, redundant barometers, compatible with ArduPilot and PX4, integrated temperature control, ports for GPS and telemetry
  • Dimensions: 38mm x 38mm mounting holes (M2.5)
  • Weight: Approximately 15 grams

FlySpark F4 V1 Flight Controller

The FlySpark F4 V1 flight controller is a high-performance controller designed for FPV drones. It features advanced sensor fusion, adaptive PID tuning, and AI-based signal processing, making it ideal for both beginner and advanced pilots.

General Hardware Details

  • Processor: STMicroelectronics STM32F405RGT6
  • IMU: InvenSense ICM-42688-P
  • Barometer: Infineon DPS310XTSA1
  • OSD Chip: Maxim Integrated MAX7456EUI+
  • ESC Stack: 60A BLS
